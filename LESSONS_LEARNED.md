## 🎓 CORE LESSONS FROM THE ARCHITECT (User)

### Lesson 0: The Vision Itself
> Build an AI civilization where agents work independently AND collaboratively,
> self-improve continuously, learn from mistakes, trade 24/7 with high WR + high R:R.

### Lesson 1: Winrate Through Filtering
Direct directional prediction = 50-65% accuracy max.
But: through filtering (high conviction, multi-TF confluence, asymmetric R:R)
→ effective winrate on SELECTED trades = 75-85%.

### Lesson 2: Multi-TF Confluence
When 1m + 3m + 5m + 15m all align → winrate jumps to 70-80%.
**Action:** Only trade full confluence setups.

### Lesson 3: Hybrid Pattern C
Independent + collaborative > master-slave OR chaos.

### Lesson 4: Diversity in Population
5 RSI agents agreeing = no information.
5 different-philosophy agents agreeing = high confidence.

### Lesson 5: Self-Creation is Core
Owner creates agents dynamically. NO pre-defined strategies.
Each agent decides: data focus, features, decision logic, risk profile.

### Lesson 6: 3000 Agents All Active 24/7
No tiered standby/archive. All 3000 vote on every consensus.

### Lesson 7: Risk Management > Strategy
Even great strategy + bad risk mgmt = bankruptcy.
**Action:** 10 hardcoded guardrails. Cannot be overridden.

### Lesson 8: Mistakes Are Data
Every loss contains information.
**Action:** Self-improvement loop mandatory for every agent.

## 🚨 CRITICAL REMINDERS

1. **Guardrails are sacred** — never bypass, even with good reason
2. **Disagreement = no trade** — don't force consensus
3. **Patience over activity** — better to skip than over-trade
4. **Multi-TF confluence required** — single TF signal = low conviction
5. **Asymmetric R:R** — winners bigger than losers
6. **Document everything** — every decision logged
7. **The user is the architect** — when uncertain, ask
